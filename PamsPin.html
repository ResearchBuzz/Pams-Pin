Easy Location-Based Twitter Search: Pam's Pin

Pam's Pin takes the address you specify and translates it into a location-based Twitter search URL which opens in a new tab. You can search for tweets in a radius of up to 100 km. Leave the checkbox ticked if you want to restrict your results to tweets from verified Twitter accounts only.













(If you're restricting your results to verified accounts only, you might need to widen your search radius from the default of 1.)




Pam's Pin was inspired by Pam Baker!
